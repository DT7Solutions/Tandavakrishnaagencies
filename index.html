About Us

Tandavakrishna Agencies is a well-known business in the electrical and building material industry. They specialize in electric PVC pipes and are one of the leading dealers in this field. In addition to PVC pipes, they also offer a wide range of tiles, sanitaryware, ceramic tiles, pipes, and other building materials from well-known brands such as Finolex, Kajaria, and Cera. Their commitment to providing high-quality products and excellent customer service has earned them a solid reputation in the industry.
